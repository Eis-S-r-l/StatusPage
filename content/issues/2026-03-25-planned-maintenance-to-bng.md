We will be performing planned updates on our two Broadband Network Gateway (BNG) routers to ensure continued network stability and performance.

### Expected Impact

To prevent downtime, these updates will be performed sequentially—meaning we will only update one router at a time. Because of this redundancy, the overall impact on service will be minimal.

* Brief Reconnection: If your equipment is actively connected to the router being updated, your session will briefly drop.
* Automatic Failover: Your modem or router will automatically re-establish its PPPoE session with our secondary, active router.
* No Action Needed: You do not need to make any configuration changes or manually intervene during this process.



> Note: If you experience an extended loss of connectivity after the scheduled maintenance window has concluded, please try power-cycling your modem/router before contacting support.
